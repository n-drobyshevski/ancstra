# AI Research

Ancstra includes an AI-powered research assistant that helps you discover new records, analyze evidence, and extend your family tree.

## Research Workspace

The research workspace is your hub for active investigations. Each workspace tracks:

- **Subject person** — who you are researching
- **Research questions** — what you want to find out
- **Gathered evidence** — records, documents, and extracted facts
- **Proposed connections** — AI-suggested relationships awaiting your review

## Starting a Research Session

1. Select a person on the tree and click **Research**
2. The AI assistant reviews the person's existing data and suggests research directions
3. Choose a direction or ask your own question
4. The assistant searches available sources and presents findings

## Evidence Analysis

When the assistant finds a potential record match, it presents:

- **Source details** — where the record came from
- **Extracted facts** — names, dates, places parsed from the record
- **Confidence score** — how likely the match is correct
- **Conflicts** — any discrepancies with your existing data

## Fact Extraction and Source Promotion

Facts extracted from research can be promoted into your tree:

1. Review the extracted fact and its source citation
2. Click **Accept** to add it as a confirmed fact, or **Reject** to dismiss
3. Accepted facts create proper source citations automatically

All AI-discovered relationships are added as `proposed_relationships` — they never modify your tree directly until you explicitly approve them.
