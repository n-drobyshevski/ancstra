# Collaboration

Share your family tree with relatives and collaborate on research together.

## Roles

Ancstra uses role-based access control with four levels:

| Role | View Tree | Edit Persons | Manage Members | Delete Tree |
|------|-----------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Viewer** | Yes | No | No | No |
| **Editor** | Yes | Yes | No | No |
| **Admin** | Yes | Yes | Yes | No |
| **Owner** | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |

- **Viewer** — read-only access. Living persons are automatically redacted.
- **Editor** — can add and edit persons, events, sources, and relationships.
- **Admin** — can invite/remove members and change roles (except owner).
- **Owner** — full control. Each tree has exactly one owner.

## Invitations

1. Open **Settings > Members** on your tree
2. Click **Invite** and enter the person's email address
3. Choose a role (viewer, editor, or admin)
4. The invitee receives an email with a link to join

Pending invitations can be revoked before they are accepted.

## Moderation Queue

Edits made by editors can optionally go through a moderation queue. When enabled, an admin or owner must approve changes before they appear on the shared tree. This is useful for large collaborative projects where data quality matters.

## Activity Feed

The activity feed shows a chronological log of all changes to the tree — who added or edited a person, merged a record, or accepted a research suggestion. Filter by member or action type to find specific changes.
