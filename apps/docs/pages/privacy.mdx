# Privacy Notice

**This is beta software. Data practices may change.**

- **Local-first:** By default, your data stays on your device in a SQLite database.
- **Web mode:** When shared online, data is stored on Turso (managed SQLite).
- **Living persons:** Persons born within 100 years with no death record are presumed living and redacted for viewers.
- **AI features:** When using biography generation or the AI research assistant, person data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing.
- **No data sales:** Your genealogy data is never sold to third parties.
