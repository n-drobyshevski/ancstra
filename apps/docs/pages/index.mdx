# Welcome to Ancstra

Ancstra is an AI-powered personal genealogy app that helps you build, research, and share your family tree.

## Quick Start

1. [Create an account](/getting-started) and import your GEDCOM file
2. [Explore your tree](/tree-view) with the interactive canvas
3. [Use AI research](/research) to discover new records and connections
4. [Invite family members](/collaboration) to contribute
5. [Export your work](/export) as GEDCOM or PDF
